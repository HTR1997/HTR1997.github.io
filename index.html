Triangles and PI

Most of you will have first learnt about Sine and Cosine in a geometry or a pre-calc class. In this context the sin and cos functions are used to describe the ratio of a leg of a right triangle and its hypotenuse. This one of the uses of sin and cos and is very "to the point" with it. These classes often start by using degrees as the unit for the trig functions but eventualy move to using radians. At this point PI becomes involved in what might feel like a very arbitrary way.
