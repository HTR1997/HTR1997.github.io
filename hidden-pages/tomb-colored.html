Trigonometry
-The study of triangles.
I never really understood trig until it was taught using circle.
Remembering opp over hyp and using socatoah never made any sense.
Teaching in this way is like giving a surface reading of a book.
Too much is left out for the reader to understand what the plot is.


Black lines are unit length.
Red lines have length sine.
Blue lines have length cosine.

Orangle lines have length cosecant (or 1/sine).
Light blue lines have length secant (or 1/cosine).

Purple lines have length tangent (or sine/cosine).
Light purple lines have length of cotangent (or 1/tangent).
